How It Works

  1. Configure your trip details and emergency contacts below
  2. Add contacts to the "emergency" group (or your chosen group)
  3. Activate the switch before your trip
  4. Check in regularly (click "I'm OK") within your interval
  5. If you miss a check-in, the alert email fires automatically
  6. The switch deactivates after firing (one-time alert)

The app checks every 15 minutes while running. For reliability on a real trip, deploy this app to a server that stays online (see README).
